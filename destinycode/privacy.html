Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

LYS Universal ("we", "us", "our") operates the Destiny Code mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

Destiny Code collects the following information, stored locally on your device:

  • Name or nickname — for personalization only
  • Birth date, birth time, birth place — used to calculate astrological charts and divination results
  • Gender — for content personalization
  • Interests and preferences — to customize your experience

This data is stored on your device using local storage and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google) — anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (processes purchase data through Apple)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier only; uses device advertising identifier with your consent)

These services may collect anonymous device information as described in their respective privacy policies.

App Tracking Transparency

We request your permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may decline without any impact on app functionality.

Data Retention

Your personal data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all stored data. We do not maintain any server-side database of user information.

Children's Privacy

Destiny Code is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
